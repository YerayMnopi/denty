## Executive Summary

Denty represents a paradigm shift in dental clinic management, transitioning from traditional booking platforms to conversational AI agents. This research plan outlines comprehensive user research methodologies to validate our hypothesis that Spanish dental clinics and their patients prefer conversational interfaces over traditional form-based systems.

Our **North Star Metric** is **Patient Booking Completion Rate via Conversation** ‚Äî the percentage of patients who successfully complete appointment bookings through conversational interfaces (WhatsApp, Instagram, web chat) without abandoning to traditional methods.

## Usability Test Plan

### Test Objectives

**Primary Objectives:**
1. Validate that patients can successfully complete appointment bookings through conversational interfaces
2. Assess clinic staff's ability to manage appointments and settings through conversational admin
3. Identify friction points in the onboarding process for new clinics
4. Evaluate the effectiveness of AI responses in handling common patient inquiries

**Secondary Objectives:**
1. Compare user preference between conversational and traditional form-based booking
2. Assess trust and confidence levels in AI-driven appointment scheduling
3. Identify opportunities for improving the conversational flow and AI personality
4. Validate information architecture and navigation patterns

### Methodology

**Approach:** Moderated usability testing with think-aloud protocol  
**Duration:** 90 minutes per session  
**Location:** Remote via Zoom + screen sharing for web tasks, phone for WhatsApp  
**Sample Size:** 32 participants (8 per persona)  
**Timeline:** 3 weeks testing period

### Test Tasks

#### For Patients (Ana Persona)

**Task 1: Discover and Initial Contact**
- *Scenario:* You need a dental cleaning and found this clinic's WhatsApp number. Make initial contact and ask for information.
- *Success Criteria:* Initiates WhatsApp conversation and receives relevant service information within 2 minutes

**Task 2: Book First Appointment**
- *Scenario:* Based on the information received, book a cleaning appointment for next week.
- *Success Criteria:* Successfully completes booking with preferred date/time, receives confirmation

**Task 3: Reschedule Appointment**
- *Scenario:* Something came up and you need to reschedule your appointment to a different day.
- *Success Criteria:* Successfully modifies appointment through conversational interface

**Task 4: Get Pre-Visit Information**
- *Scenario:* You want to know what to expect during your cleaning appointment and if there's anything you should prepare.
- *Success Criteria:* Receives relevant pre-visit instructions and preparation details

#### For Clinic Staff (Mar√≠a & Carmen Personas)

**Task 5: Review Daily Schedule**
- *Scenario:* It's Monday morning. Check today's appointments and identify any issues or special requirements.
- *Success Criteria:* Successfully accesses and interprets daily schedule information

**Task 6: Handle Schedule Change**
- *Scenario:* Dr. L√≥pez needs to leave early today. Block his schedule from 3 PM onwards and notify affected patients.
- *Success Criteria:* Successfully blocks time slots and triggers patient notifications

**Task 7: Update Service Information**
- *Scenario:* The clinic is offering a new teeth whitening promotion. Update the service description and pricing.
- *Success Criteria:* Successfully updates service information through conversational interface

**Task 8: Access Weekly Performance Data**
- *Scenario:* Prepare a weekly summary for the clinic owner including key metrics and any concerning trends.
- *Success Criteria:* Retrieves relevant analytics and can interpret main KPIs

#### For New Clinic Onboarding (Carmen Persona)

**Task 9: Complete Initial Setup**
- *Scenario:* You've signed up for Denty and need to configure your clinic for the first time.
- *Success Criteria:* Completes onboarding conversation with clinic details in <25 minutes

**Task 10: Review First Patient Booking**
- *Scenario:* A patient has booked through your new system. Review the booking details and understand the notification process.
- *Success Criteria:* Successfully accesses booking information and understands workflow

## UX Metrics Framework

### North Star Metric üåü

**Patient Booking Completion Rate via Conversation: 85%**

*Definition:* The percentage of patients who successfully complete appointment bookings through conversational interfaces (WhatsApp, Instagram, web chat) without abandoning to traditional methods (phone, in-person).

*Formula:* 
```
(Completed Conversational Bookings) / (Total Booking Attempts via Conversation) √ó 100
```

**Why This Metric?**

1. **Direct Value Alignment:** Measures the core value proposition‚Äîthat conversational booking is superior to traditional methods
2. **Business Impact:** Higher completion rates directly correlate with increased revenue and reduced operational costs
3. **User-Centric:** Reflects actual user behavior and preference, not just technical functionality
4. **Actionable:** Provides clear direction for product improvements and optimization efforts
5. **Measurable:** Can be tracked accurately across all conversational channels

This metric encapsulates Denty's fundamental promise: making dental appointments as easy as sending a message to a friend.

### Supporting Metrics (Input Metrics)

**Conversation Quality Metrics:**
- AI Response Accuracy Rate: >90%

## Competitive UX Analysis

### Direct Competitors

#### Doctoralia (DocPlanner)
**UX Strengths:**
- Comprehensive doctor profiles with reviews and ratings
- Visual calendar interface with clear availability
- Mobile app with good performance
- Multi-language support across European markets

**UX Weaknesses:**
- Heavy reliance on forms and dropdowns
- No conversational interface
- Complex navigation for simple booking tasks
- Limited personalization and follow-up automation

**Key Differentiator:** Denty's conversational approach eliminates form fatigue and provides instant responses vs Doctoralia's traditional web interface.

#### Gesden (Practice Management)
**UX Strengths:**
- Comprehensive patient record management
- Integrated billing and treatment planning
- Familiar interface for dental professionals
- Robust reporting and analytics

**UX Weaknesses:**
- Steep learning curve for new users
- No patient-facing booking interface
- Limited mobile optimization
- Outdated visual design

**Key Differentiator:** Denty provides modern patient engagement while Gesden focuses on internal practice management.

### Indirect Competitors

#### Traditional Phone Booking
**Advantages:**
- Human touch and empathy
- Complex problem-solving capability
- Familiar process for older demographics
- Immediate clarification of concerns

**Disadvantages:**
- Limited to business hours
- Wait times and busy signals
- Staff costs and training requirements
- Inconsistent information delivery

**Denty's Edge:** 24/7 availability with consistent, accurate responses and instant booking confirmation.

#### Generic Booking Platforms (Calendly, Acuity)
**Advantages:**
- Simple, clean interfaces
- Good calendar integration
- Automated confirmations and reminders
- Reasonable pricing

**Disadvantages:**
- Generic, not healthcare-optimized
- Limited conversational capability
- No industry-specific features
- Minimal patient relationship management

**Denty's Edge:** Healthcare-specific conversational AI with patient CRM and clinic management integration.

### Emerging Trends Analysis

#### Voice-First Interfaces
- Growing adoption of voice assistants in healthcare
- Opportunity for future Denty voice integration
- Current limitation: Most dental bookings still prefer text-based privacy

#### AI-Powered Healthcare Tools
- Increasing patient acceptance of AI in healthcare
- Regulatory scrutiny requiring transparency and reliability
- Competitive advantage: Early adoption with proven conversational AI

#### Omnichannel Patient Engagement
- Patients expect consistent experience across touchpoints
- Integration between social media, websites, and messaging apps
- Denty advantage: Native omnichannel design from day one

### Competitive Positioning

**Denty's Unique Value Proposition:**
1. **Conversational-First:** Only solution designed for natural language interaction
2. **Spanish-Optimized:** Purpose-built for Spanish dental market needs
3. **AI-Native:** Not a traditional system with AI bolted on
4. **Comprehensive:** Booking + CRM + Website + Social in one platform
5. **Clinic-Specific:** Each clinic gets its own AI agent with personalized responses

**Market Gaps Addressed:**
- Lack of modern patient engagement in Spanish dental market
- No comprehensive conversational AI solutions for healthcare
- Limited integration between patient communication and practice management
- Poor mobile experience in existing dental software

**Competitive Risks:**
- Large players (Doctoralia) could add conversational features

**Mitigation Strategy:**

- Develop deep healthcare domain knowledge in AI responses
- Establish strong brand recognition in Spanish dental community
